# Omnis - Voidborne Language Unlock

event "omnis language unlock void"
	set "language: Voidborne"

mission "Omnis: Unlock Voidborne Language"
	name "Unlock Voidborne Language"
	description "Instantly learn the Voidborne language through Omnis debug tools."
	job
	landing
	to offer
		government "Omnis"
		not "language: Voidborne"
	on offer
		conversation
			`Omnis Debug Interface - Language Module`
			`Target language: Voidborne (Vo Etti dialect)`
			`Status: Locked`
			``
			`Would you like to unlock the Voidborne language? This will allow you to understand Voidborne hails and communications.`
			choice
				`	"Yes, unlock the language."`
					accept
				`	"Not right now."`
					decline
	on accept
		event "language unlock void"
		conversation
			`Omnis Debug Interface - Language Module`
			`Target language: Voidborne`
			`Status: Unlocked`
			``
			`The Voidborne language has been unlocked. Key phrases:`
			`  "Vo Et" - Who are you? / Pass by`
			`  "Ai Shik" - We see you / Acknowledged`
			`  "Berideqe" - Warning / Stand down`
			`  "Anad" - Death / The end`
			``
			`You will now understand Voidborne hails and transmissions.`

# Omnis - Devourer Telepathic Link

event "omnis telepathy unlock devourer"
	set "language: Devourer"

mission "Omnis: Establish Devourer Telepathic Link"
	name "Establish Devourer Telepathic Link"
	description "Use Omnis to tune into the Devourer hive mind and understand their telepathic communications."
	landing
	to offer
		government "Omnis"
		not "language: Devourer"
	on offer
		conversation
			`Omnis Debug Interface - Psionic Module`
			`Target species: Devourer Swarm (Armensajdalnakaulaa)`
			`Telepathic Status: Unlinked`
			``
			`WARNING: Establishing a direct psionic link to the Devourer hive mind is extremely dangerous. The Devourers do not communicate through language - they project pure thought, emotion, and hunger directly into the minds of their prey.`
			``
			`Omnis can filter the signal and translate the concepts into comprehensible language, but the underlying hunger will remain. You will feel what they feel.`
			``
			`Are you certain you wish to proceed?`
			choice
				`	"Yes. Establish the link."`
					accept
				`	"No. I don't need that in my head."`
					decline
	on accept
		event "omnis telepathy unlock devourer"
		conversation
			`Omnis Debug Interface - Psionic Module`
			`Target species: Devourer Swarm (Armensajdalnakaulaa)`
			`Telepathic Status: Linked`
			``
			`The link snaps into place.`
			``
			`For a moment, you feel EVERYTHING. The hunger of a trillion minds. The taste of dying stars. The endless, aching need to consume. Your hands shake. Your heart races.`
			``
			`Then the filters engage. The hunger recedes to a dull ache at the back of your skull. You can hear them now - not words, but meaning. Intent.`
			``
			`"Prey." "Consume." "More." "Not finished." "We are endless."`
			``
			`You understand the Devourers now. You wish you didn't.`
			``
			`The link is permanent. You can hear them whenever they're near. Sleep well, Captain.`
