# Voidborne Plugin - Jobs

mission "Voidborne: Learn the Language"
	name "Learn the Voidborne Language"
	description "Spend time studying the Voidborne language to understand their hails and communications."
	job
	landing
	source "Void Anchor"
	to offer
		has "Voidborne: The Observation: done"
		not "language: Voidborne"
	on offer
		conversation
			`A Voidborne scholar approaches you at the spaceport. Unlike the constructs, this one seems almost eager. "You have spoken with the Collective," it says. "But you do not understand us. Not truly. I can teach you our language - our words, our meanings. It will take time and focus. Are you willing to learn?"`
			choice
				`	"Yes, teach me."`
					accept
				`	"Not right now."`
					decline
	on accept
		event "language unlock void"
		conversation
			`After weeks of study, the Voidborne language begins to make sense. "Vo Et" - pass by, leave in peace. "Ai Shik" - I see you, acknowledged. "Berideqe" - a warning, stand down. "Anad" - death, the end. You now understand the Voidborne when they speak. The scholar nods. "You learn quickly, for an outsider. Go now. Speak our words."`
